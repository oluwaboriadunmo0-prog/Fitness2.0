Goal tracks

Built around three real goals

Muscle gain

Train heavier, eat more

Higher-protein, higher-calorie meals like ofada rice with chicken, paired with compound lifts like squats and deadlifts.

Fat loss

Stay full, stay lean

Fibre-rich, lower-calorie meals like beans porridge and pepper soup, paired with metabolic circuits and intervals.

Maintenance

Consistency over intensity

A balanced plate and a varied, moderate training split designed to be sustainable for the long run.
